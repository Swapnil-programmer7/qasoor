Overview

Qasoor marks the start of a beautiful chapter in the lives of it's makers.

3 months ago, in march the Governors of Technology Filmmaking and Photography Society, broke the news that the new members are required to make a movie all by themselves.

At that time for once everyone doubted their capabilities. We were questioning ourselves and yet in June after having completed the movie, we feel a great sense of joy within us.

Making this movie was a challenging yet cherishable experience. There were many hurdles sure but with constant help and support of the team members, every difficulty was overcome.

The team feels elated having completed this amazing feat. Congratulations!!
